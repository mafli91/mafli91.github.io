About the game...


People say if you call his name five times in the mirror he appears.

These two fairies appear whether you like it or not.

Curvy girls, dry humour, kitsch, poor grammar, monsters and poor wig choices - a game for everyone's liking.

Enchanting heroines are out for an adventure where game is just means for a short comic story. It's not for children.

On offer is 13 levels including 4 comic story levels, 3 levels with a basic puzzle solving, 5 driven by collecting fairy dust and a boss fight. Most music and all artwork is my own.

From a technical side game offers casual GUI with top menu offering help where you can read about game, enemies, controls and change volume. On the right side is a panel with a minimap on the bottom, above a player tips, above buttons to swap levels (they are locked unless you use encoded cheats or reach given level) and at the top are buttons to swap between heroines.

You control game using both buttons and scrollbar of the mouse and keyboard: Q,W,E,A,S,D,N, SPACEBAR, SHIFT, ENTER.

Enemies have randomised behaviour depending on timers, random number generator, life count, proximity to the player and items. Above that boss have a range of spells to attack the player and states of temporal immunity to damage.

Game also introduces saving and loading with File Chooser as well as handling corrupted files: both images and music.

At the end you get to sit through short credits scene.
